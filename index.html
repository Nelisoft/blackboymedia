Black Boy Media

Black Boy Media is a mission-driven media and storytelling company centered on empowering underrepresented voices, particularly Black creatives, in the entertainment industry. Through its groundbreaking mentorship initiative, the Black Boy Writes / Black Girl Writes Program, the company equips emerging writers with industry access, professional development, and personalized support. This is accomplished by: a) a robust mentorship framework led by established writers, showrunners, and producers, and b) a growing portfolio of original content, collaborations, and strategic partnerships that spotlight authentic, untold stories across film and television.
